How do I get there?

You have 4 options on how to travel to Mykonos:

  1. Take the ferry from the port in Athens (Rafina or Pireas), which usually stops in Santorini and Paros.
  2. Fly in from Athens or other European cities to the Mykonos International Airport.
  3. Take a smaller boat from Paros or Naxos.
  4. Take a helicopter from Santorini.

Where Should I Stay?

If you are looking to stay in Xora i would recommend staying at Pnoe. The Residence Pnoe rests in the most exclusive location of Mykonos Town with breathtaking panoramic views of the old port.
If you are interested in staying in a villa outside of Mykonos Town, Cape Ftelia is perfect for you. The luxury villa, Cape Ftelia, is located in the town of Ftelia approximately 15 minutes by car from Mykonos town and the international airport.
