How do I get there?

You have 4 options on how to travel to Mykonos:

  1. Take the ferry from the port in Athens (Rafina or Pireas), which usually stops at Santorini and Paros.
  2. Fly in from Athens or other European cities to the Mykonos International Airport.
  3. Take a smaller boat from Paros or Naxos.
  4. Take a helicopter from Santorini.
